How our web content will provide solutions for our audience

Team IQ must appeal to a broad spectrum of users: our client (NAIT), website users, and our presentation audience. We have considered what may promote interest in the DMIT program and will discuss three significant categories: quality, career outcomes, and technology in learning.

The DMIT program at NAIT offers high-quality education in a 2-year diploma, reducing the overall tuition costs. By contrast, other colleges and universities in the Edmonton community often require a 4-year bachelor’s degree for a similar education. The DMIT program is fine-tuned to deliver industry-relevant content without extra padding that may not always apply to the real workplace.

Additionally, labour market outcomes for the DMIT program are extremely positive. NAIT students can expect an “87% employment rate within 9 months of graduating from full-time programs” (Quick Facts, 2021). As a result, graduates of DMIT can be confident they will find a career in their chosen path.

Technology is a major component of all streams in the DMIT program. Anyone who is drawn to technology and enthusiastic about what they can accomplish with it will find many stimulating aspects of DMIT. Working with technology appeals strongly to a lot of people, and DMIT provides a number of paths to those types of careers.
